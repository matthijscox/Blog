Scientific Coder

I am moving my professional content off this private blog and onto scientificcoder.com. This way I can keep my weird philosophies separate from my work. Also I might want to bundle my professional stories into a book one day.

The benefit of the new blog is that it has a newsletter you can subscribe to. I love this little static Franklin.jl (Jekyll based) website I have here, but it's a bit tedious to work with (especially when writing on mobile) and difficult to add all these modern features.

If you are a computational scientist or software developer, I hope to see you on The Scientific Coder! If not, feel free to stay on this blog. Functional Noise has an RSS feed that you can subscribe to, if you know how.
